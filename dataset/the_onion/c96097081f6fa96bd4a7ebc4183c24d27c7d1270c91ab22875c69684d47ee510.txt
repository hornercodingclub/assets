Doritos has announced a limited-edition online release of "Doritos Rainbows," a bag containing red, orange, green, blue, and purple chips in a nod to the LGBT movement's rainbow flag, available to consumers who pledge $10 or more to the It Gets Better Project, which aims to improve the lives of LGBT youth.
