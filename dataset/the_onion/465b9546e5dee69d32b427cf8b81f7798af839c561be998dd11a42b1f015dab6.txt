The Girl Scouts of America announced that though scouts have always sold cookies face-to-face to encourage salesmanship, the organization will now allow them to sell the popular cookies online to "use modern tools to expand the size and scope of their cookie business." What do think? "Good. This should help some of the shyer girls become more comfortable talking to strangers online."
