EL PASO, TX--Praising its characters, story, and ending, Maya Brown, a freshman at DeSoto High School, decided this Monday that William Shakespeare's classic might just be her favorite play. "I love how romantic it is, and especially how well the dialogue is written," said the 15-year-old, whose knowledge of theater and the dramatic arts reportedly dates back to the fall of 2012. "Shakespeare is just such an amazing playwright. He's probably one of the best in history." Brown added that if she were ever in a production of , she would want to play Juliet.
