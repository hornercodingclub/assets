Meanwhile, 54 pupils and five staff at Ysgol Clawdd Offa, Prestatyn, will also have to isolate after a case was linked to the site. Denbighshire council said both schools remained open and measures were in place to protect staff and pupils. The council said it was working closely with the schools, Public Health Wales and NHS Wales Test, Trace and Protect. It said parents and guardians had been informed. Those advised to self-isolate should book a coronavirus test if they develop a new or continuous cough, a high temperature or a loss of or change to sense of smell or taste, it added.
