Colin McCourt, 40, from Dumbarton, was pronounced dead at the scene of the collision with a car on Tuesday afternoon. The crash happened on the A814, half a mile west of Cardross, at about 16:40. The road was closed for several hours for a collision investigation. Police said a 44-year-old man had been arrested in connection with road traffic offences. They said he was due to appear in Dumbarton Sheriff Court on Wednesday.
