Sussex Police said officers were called to the middle of Chichester at about 17:30 GMT on Thursday. Two boys, both aged 12, were arrested on suspicion of attempted robbery. The pair, from Chichester, have been released on bail until 12 February while the investigation continues, a police spokesman said.
