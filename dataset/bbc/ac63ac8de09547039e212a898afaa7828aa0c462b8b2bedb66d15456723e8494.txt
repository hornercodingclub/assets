Joe Whitchurch died in hospital three days after he was stabbed at about 05:30 GMT in Hickings Lane in Stapleford, Nottinghamshire. Jake Rollinson, 20, of Hickings Lane, denied murder and perverting the course of justice when he appeared at Nottingham Crown Court on Monday. Lorna Richardson, 25, of Blake Road in Stapleford, also denied perverting the course of justice. A trial date is yet to be confirmed, and the next hearing is set to take place on 12 March.
