Trials have shown the Wrexham vaccine to be between 62% and 90% effective in protecting against Covid-19. It is the cheapest of the vaccines being considered, and can be stored at normal fridge temperatures. But the vaccine from Pfizer/BioNTech is also being scrutinised by regulators, and could also get early approval. Moderna are also hoping to get the go-ahead in the US and Europe for its vaccine. The UK has already pre-ordered doses of all three vaccines: Older, more vulnerable people and care home staff will be amongst the first to receive the vaccines when they are approved.
