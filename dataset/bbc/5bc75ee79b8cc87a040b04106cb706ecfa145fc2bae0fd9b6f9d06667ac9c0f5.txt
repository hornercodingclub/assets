There were also 570 new Covid infections reported, taking the total number of cases to 189,152. Nearly 19,000 people received their first coronavirus vaccine dose, bringing a return to vaccination levels of last week following Sunday's drop. It takes the total number vaccinated to 289,566 people, while 581 have had their second jab. Wrexham has the highest rolling seven-day case rate in Wales with 539.1 people per 100,000 population testing positive. That is followed by Flintshire with 402.9 and Denbighshire with 280.1. Ceredigion has the lowest case rate at 108.7 per 100,000 people, followed by Monmouthshire with 139.5. The Wales average is 219 per 100,000. More than 2.365m Covid tests have been carried out in Wales, with 1,486,278 people tested and a total of 7,201 tests were carried out on Monday.
