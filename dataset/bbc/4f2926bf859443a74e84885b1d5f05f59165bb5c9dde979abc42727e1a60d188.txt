Applicants from six Bristol constituencies would have had to send him a photo of their ballot paper as proof of their vote against the Tories to get what was termed "a souvenir piece". Police said on Monday: "It is a criminal offence under the Representation of the People Act 1983 for any voter to accept or agree to accept a gift or similar in return for voting or refraining from voting. "Any person participating in an offer to receive a gift is at risk of being prosecuted."
