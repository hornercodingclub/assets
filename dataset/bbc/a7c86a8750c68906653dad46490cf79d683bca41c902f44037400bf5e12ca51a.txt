Suffolk Trading Standards discovered 500 of the fake versions of Fingerlings - - at the Port of Felixstowe. It said the toys, which were due for a warehouse in Dagenham, have gone for testing to see if they are hazardous. The seizure follows raids on an Ipswich shop where 141 fake Fingerlings were found. Matthew Hicks, Suffolk County Council's cabinet member for environment and public protection, said: "Christmas is a time of joy and giving but after finding these large quantities of counterfeit toys we are warning people to check before they buy."
