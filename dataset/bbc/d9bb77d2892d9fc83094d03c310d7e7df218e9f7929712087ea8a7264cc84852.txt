Synthetic opioid fentanyl is 100 times more potent than morphine and just 0.002 grams mixed with heroin can kill. South Wales Police said the discovery was made after a drug addict tested positive for the substance following a non-fatal overdose. Det Insp Stuart Johnson said: "I urge all drug users in the local area to be cautious." A force spokesman said the batch of heroin was found in Neath Port Talbot, which has the highest rates of drug deaths in Wales and the second highest in the UK.
