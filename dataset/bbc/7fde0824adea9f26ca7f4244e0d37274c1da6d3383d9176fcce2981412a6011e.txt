Hundreds of doses arrived on Friday, with vaccinations starting immediately. Care home residents and staff, frontline health workers and those aged over 80 are the first being offered the vaccine. The islands are following the priority groups set by the (JCVI). St Mary's based GP Dr Chris Picken said islanders are "delighted at the arrival of the vaccine." He added: "Our aim is to offer the vaccination to the first four priority groups by the middle of the week. "While the arrival of the vaccine offers the hope that we can start to return to the path of normality we know how, with our small population, now more than ever we cannot be complacent."
