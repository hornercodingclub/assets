The blue Peugeot 207 crashed on Ballybogey Road near Ballymoney at about 06:00 GMT on Friday. Police closed Ballybogey Road at its junctions with Frosses Road and Tullaghgore Road while they investigated the crash but it has since reopened. They have appealed for anyone who witnessed the incident to contact them.
