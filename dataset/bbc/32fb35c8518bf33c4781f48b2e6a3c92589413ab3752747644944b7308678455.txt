The one-vehicle accident happened southbound on the A90, between Balmedie and Newburgh, at about 07:40. The driver was taken to the Aberdeen Royal Infirmary, where he was pronounced dead a short time later. Relatives have been informed. Police Scotland, said the road was closed at the Newburgh junction to allow an investigation. Sgt Scott Deans said: "I am appealing to any motorists who were on the road around the time of the incident to contact us. In particular, I would ask anyone with a dashcam to check it as the footage may be able to assist our inquiries. "A man has lost his life and it's imperative we find out how it happened."
