Speaking at the time, he said he was "very conscious" a lot of his duties would be virtual. "The socially-distanced element will no doubt be very difficult, but it is something I see as an opportunity to show the type of leadership that is needed and required over this difficult period," he said. He plans to chair the full Derry City and Strabane District Council meeting scheduled for Thursday via YouTube and has encouraged the public to continue adhering to public health guidance.
