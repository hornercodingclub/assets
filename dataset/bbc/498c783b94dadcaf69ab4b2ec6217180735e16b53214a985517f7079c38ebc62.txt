The increase from 1 January 2021 includes a PS5 rise in the annual charge to PS90 and rises for "pay as you throw" stickers. The cost of 90-litre black bag sticker will rise by 20p to PS2.70, with a 10p increase to PS1.50 for a 50-litre sticker. States members voted 33-3 against the move with one abstention. It means the cost for household rubbish disposal in an average weekly household has been estimated to rise by 20p to PS4.60. Disposal is paid for by the annual charge and by islanders purchasing and attaching a sticker to each black bin bag. In 2019, 73% of Guernsey's household waste was recycled, up from half in 2017 and already ahead of the target of 70% set for 2030.
