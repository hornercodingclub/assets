The rave at Cadover Bridge on Dartmoor, Devon, was reported on Friday morning. Two people have been arrested and hundreds of cars were found parked near stages set up in a quarry, police said. Officers also found stolen nitrous oxide canisters and a large amount of waste, including human excrement, in the surrounding area. Closed roads have since reopened. Sgt Rob Wilson, from Devon and Cornwall Police, said it was a large-scale illegal party and "not a place for a peaceful post-Christmas dog walk". Ch Supt Matt Longman said the force had been concerned "both for the people in attendance and the impact on the local community."
