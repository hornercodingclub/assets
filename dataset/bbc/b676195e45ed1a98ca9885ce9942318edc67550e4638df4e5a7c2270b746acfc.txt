The Scottish Fire and Rescue Service (SRFRS) said it was alerted to the blaze at Bredero House in the Imperial Docks just before 12:20. A large plume of smoke could be seen and four appliances were sent  to the scene. Forth Ports said the fire had broken out in the engine bay of a piece of machinery in a storage yard. It said no-one was hurt. Police Scotland said it was aware of the incident but not involved.
