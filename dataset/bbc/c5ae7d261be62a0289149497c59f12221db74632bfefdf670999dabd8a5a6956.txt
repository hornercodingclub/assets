The fountain, in Victoria Square, has not been working since 2013. Installed in 1993, it was given a PS300,000 refurbishment in 2010, PS70,000 was spent on repairs in 2009, and about PS40,000 in 2006. Birmingham City Council said it wants to make the square "as attractive as possible" until it is fixed. Planting is due to be completed in mid-July. A full feasibility study is due to begin in January 2016, to work out what is wrong with the fountain and how much repairs will cost.
