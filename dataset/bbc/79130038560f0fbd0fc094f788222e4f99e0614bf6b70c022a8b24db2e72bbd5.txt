According to the latest figures, the case rate in the week to 21 January in Luton had fallen to 658.1 cases per 100,000 people - the 16th-highest for a district in England. All districts in Bedfordshire, Buckinghamshire, Hertfordshire, had a week-on-week fall in case rates. In Cambridgeshire, Fenland had a small week-on-week rise in case rates as did Kettering in Northamptonshire, two of only 27 districts in England to see a rise.
