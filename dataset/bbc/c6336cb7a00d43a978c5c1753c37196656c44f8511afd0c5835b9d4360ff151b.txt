Marine scientist Francis Binney explained fishing trawlers accidentally catch, injure or kill dolphins when chasing shoals. He confirmed a second dolphin had been found in St Ouen's Bay. Mr Binney said Jersey sees around six marine mammal strandings every winter, due to the scarcity of food driving them inshore and likelihood of pups becoming separated from their parents.
