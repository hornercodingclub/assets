Police said a security officer was assaulted by a man armed with a crowbar on Great Victoria Street at about 08:50 GMT. Four men aged in their 40s and a 52-year-old woman were arrested shortly after in west Belfast. The security officer was not injured but was left "badly shaken". A cash box was also taken and retrieved by police. Two of the men were arrested following a collision on the Monagh Bypass. The other two men and the woman were arrested at a property on the Monagh Road. They have been arrested on suspicion of a number of offences including robbery.
