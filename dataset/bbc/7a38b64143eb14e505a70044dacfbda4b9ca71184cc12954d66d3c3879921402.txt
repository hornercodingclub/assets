Two women aged 49 and 63 were arrested on Monday evening on suspicion of offences relating to the investigation. The man who was arrested earlier is 41 years old. Police have carried out a number of searches in the area as part of their investigation. They said a number of people were present at the scene when ambulance staff arrived.
