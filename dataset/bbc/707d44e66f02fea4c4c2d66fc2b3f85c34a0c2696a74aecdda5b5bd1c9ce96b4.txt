South Wales Police were called after a boy was found unconscious at St David's Crescent in Penarth, Vale of Glamorgan, on Friday at about 20:15 GMT. He was taken to the University Hospital of Wales in Cardiff for treatment. Officers said a 19-year-old man is in custody on suspicion of assault and a 17-year-old boy is on bail. Police added that he has been "released on bail pending further enquiries". Detectives said the victim remains in a "life-threatening condition" in hospital.
