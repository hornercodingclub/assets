Read Our Latest Issue Emotion trumps reason at the ballot box Such is the conclusion of political scientist Charles Taber of Stony Brook University and his colleagues, who have examined during the past 10 years how people decide whom they vote for. "Politics, like religion and war, is all about emotions and feelings," Taber says. "The enlightenment model of dis?passionate reason as the duty of citizenship is empirically bankrupt."
