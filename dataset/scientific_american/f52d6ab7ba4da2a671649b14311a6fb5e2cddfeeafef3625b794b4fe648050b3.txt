Read Our Latest Issue Using comic books to explore the issues and history of nuclear power The Atomic Age kicked off with a bang on July 16, 1945, with the detonation of a test at the Alamogordo Test Range in the New Mexico desert, during what's known as the "Golden Age" of comic books and strips. So although it may seem odd in the 21st century to read about in the funny pages, it was perhaps inevitable during an age when both were still nascent curiosities.
