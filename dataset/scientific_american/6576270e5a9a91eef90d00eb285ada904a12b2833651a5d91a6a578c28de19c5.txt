Read Our Latest Issue Storm tracking could be scuttled by interference from next-gen wireless communications The rollout of 5G wireless technology will make mobile communications dramatically faster and more efficient. But 5G could also lead to dangerous setbacks for weather forecasting. That is the worry voiced by national and international science agencies and independent experts. The Federal Communications Commission (FCC), however, which regulates U.S. wireless networks, doesn't seem concerned--and that's a big problem. This is a preview.
