Read Our Latest Issue AI will serve our species, not control it Humans are the only animals that build machines. By doing so, we expand our capabilities beyond our biological limits. Tools turn our hands into more versatile appendages. Cars let us travel faster, and airplanes give us wings. Computers endow us with bigger brains and memory capacity, and smartphones orchestrate daily life. Now we are creating technology that can evolve on its own by encoding into it an ability to learn through data and effort. Will it ultimately supplant us?
