Read Our Latest Issue Read Our Latest Issue Recommendations from by Bob Berman Little, Brown, 2014 (($27)) Science writer Berman invites readers to consider movement in all its mundane and fascinating forms: the temperature state of absolute zero, wherein atoms nearly stop moving; water circling down a toilet drain; hurricane-force winds; the mind-boggling speed of our universe's expansion; and more. Part scientific and historical exploration, part travelogue, Berman's story re-minds us of this inescapable law of nature: "Nothing in the universe is stationary," he writes. "Absolutely everything moves."
