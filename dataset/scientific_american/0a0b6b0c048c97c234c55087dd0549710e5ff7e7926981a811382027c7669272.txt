Read Our Latest Issue Logic suggests that having options allows people to select precisely what makes them happiest. But, as studies show, abundant choice often makes for misery Americans today choose among more options in more parts of life than has ever been possible before. To an extent, it is only logical to think that if some choice is good, more is better. Yet recent research strongly suggests that, logically, this assumption is wrong.
