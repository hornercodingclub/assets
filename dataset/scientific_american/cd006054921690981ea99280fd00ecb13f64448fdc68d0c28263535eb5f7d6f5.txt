Read Our Latest Issue They are coming, but not the way you may have been led to think Soon electronic chauffeurs will take us wherever we want to go, whenever we want, in complete safety--as long as we do not need to make any left turns across traffic. Changing road surfaces are a problem, too. So are snow and ice. It will be crucial to avoid traffic cops, crossing guards and emergency vehicles. And in an urban environment where pedestrians are likely to run out in front of the car, we should probably just walk or take the subway. This is a preview.
