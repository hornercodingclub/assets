Read Our Latest Issue Clues to reading feline body language Cats have a curious allure. Even the most pampered house cats seem to flaunt their independence, as if to say that they do not really need us to get by. Despite this hauteur--or perhaps because of it--many of us cannot resist bringing these regal creatures into our homes, litter boxes and all.
