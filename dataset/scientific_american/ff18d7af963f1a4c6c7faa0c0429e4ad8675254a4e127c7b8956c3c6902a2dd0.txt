Read Our Latest Issue A long-cherished view of how and why feathers evolved has now been overturned Hair, scales, fur, feathers. Of all the body coverings nature has designed, feathers are the most various and the most mysterious. How did these incredibly strong, wonderfully lightweight, amazingly intricate appendages evolve? Where did they come from? Only in around the past two decades have we begun to answer this question. Several lines of research have converged on a remarkable conclusion: the feather evolved in dinosaurs before the appearance of birds. This is a preview.
