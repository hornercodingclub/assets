Read Our Latest Issue Viruses or bacteria may be at the root of schizophrenia and other disorders Schizophrenia is a devastating illness. One percent of the world's population suffers from its symptoms of hallucinations, psychosis and impaired cognitive ability. The disease destroys relationships and renders many of its sufferers unable to hold down a job. What could cause such frightening damage to the brain?
