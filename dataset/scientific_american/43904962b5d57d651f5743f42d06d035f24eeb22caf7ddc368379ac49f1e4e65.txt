Read Our Latest Issue Drug therapies show promise in the battle against addictive stimulants Many people still chalk up the destructive behavior of a drug addict to a lack of willpower or weakness of character. To a neuroscientist, however, drug addiction is a psychiatric illness that develops when the repeated use of narcotics disrupts brain chemistry.
