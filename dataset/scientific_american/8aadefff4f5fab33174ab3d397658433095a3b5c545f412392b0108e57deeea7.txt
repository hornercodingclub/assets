Read Our Latest Issue Drilling in Greenland to prepare for Mars The mission: to have the device, named Chronos, melt its way into Earth's ice sheet above the Arctic circle as a test of its worthiness for the coveted NASA mission to Mars in 2011. If chosen, Chronos would drill into our neighbor's northern ice cap. "Ice is probably the only accessible climate record on Mars; if there is a climate record, it is preserved in those ice caps" as variations in isotope abundance, explains lead engineer Greg Cardell.
