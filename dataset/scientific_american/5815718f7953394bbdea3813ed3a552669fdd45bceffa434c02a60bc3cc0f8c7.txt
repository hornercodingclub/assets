Read Our Latest Issue Hallucinogenic drugs, which blew minds in the 1960s, soon may be used to treat mental ailments Mind-altering psychedelics are back--but this time they are being explored in labs for their therapeutic applications rather than being used illegally. Studies are looking at these hallucinogens to treat a number of otherwise intractable psychiatric disorders, including chronic depression, post-traumatic stress disorder, and drug or alcohol dependency.
