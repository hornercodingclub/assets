Read Our Latest Issue In pursuit of evidence that life arose on Earth more than once, scientists are searching for microbes that are radically different from all known organisms The origin of life is one of the great unsolved problems of science. Nobody knows how, where or when life originated. About all that is known for certain is that microbial life had established itself on Earth by about three and a half billion years ago.
