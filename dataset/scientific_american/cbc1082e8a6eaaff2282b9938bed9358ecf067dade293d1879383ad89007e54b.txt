Read Our Latest Issue Analyses of a recently discovered type of hot vent ecosystem on the seafloor suggest new possibilities for how life evolved Few places on Earth's continents remain to be explored, and it is unlikely that many new natural wonders await discovery in some forgotten corner. But below the ocean surface is a different story. We know more about the facade of Mars than about the 75 percent of our own planet's surface that lies underwater. Untold surprises await us there.
