Read Our Latest Issue Read Our Latest Issue Books and recommendations from  by Roger Wiens Basic Books, 2013 ($25.99) The principal investigator for the ChemCam--the Curiosity rover's rock-vaporizing laser--recounts his experiences working at the frontier of space exploration. Tight deadlines and budget constraints pushed mission teams to exercise their creativity and find D.I.Y.-style solutions to problems that threatened to halt projects. Wiens offers a backstage tour of the delights and disappointments of working on missions--including the Genesis mission to collect solar-wind particles, which wound up crash-landing in the Utah desert.
