This idea could do more harm than good How many stages of life are there? According to Hindu teachings, human life unfolds in four stages: childhood, apprenticeship, adulthood and old age. William Shakespeare in insisted on seven, beginning with infancy, when we are "mewling and puking," and ending with old age, when we are "sans teeth, sans eyes, sans taste, sans everything."
