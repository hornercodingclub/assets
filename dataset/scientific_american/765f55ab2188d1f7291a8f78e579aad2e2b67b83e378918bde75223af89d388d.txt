Read Our Latest Issue The breakup may be painful, but most kids adjust well over time Many of the 1.5 million children in the U.S. whose parents divorce every year feel as if their world is falling apart. Divorcing parents are usually very concerned about the welfare of their children during this troublesome process. Some parents are so worried that they remain in unhappy marriages, believing it will protect their offspring from the trauma of divorce. This is a preview.
