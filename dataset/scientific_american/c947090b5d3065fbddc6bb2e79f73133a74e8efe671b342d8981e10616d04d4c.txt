Read Our Latest Issue British Telecom bets all on internet protocols Genius or madness? The sheer expense and scale of this project mean the stakes are high. Ralph Cochrane, director of corporate development in charge of 21CN, says it has been called both. "I think we're slowly proving we're not mad," he says. Companies such as Telecom Italia, which has an IP-based long-distance backbone, have accomplished some of what British Telecom hopes to do. "But no one else is ripping out their entire network and putting in a shiny new one based on IP," Cochrane claims. This is a preview.
