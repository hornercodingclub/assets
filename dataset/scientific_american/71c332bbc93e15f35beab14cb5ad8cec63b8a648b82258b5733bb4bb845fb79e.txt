Read Our Latest Issue Remembering the maverick physicist who pioneered an "anthropic" approach to cosmology The Barrow family's members loved Italy, where they maintained many professional and social contacts over the decades. It was in Milan that another remarkable John Barrow project culminated: the premiere of the stage play which he had written. It duly received the Premi Ubu Italian theater prize. It was thus, with some poignancy, that John and his wife Elizabeth were able to make one last trip there just a few weeks ago, in the face of onerous coronavirus-related travel restrictions and the debilitating effects of treatment for his colon cancer.
