New programs are changing that As young scientists work their way through graduate school and postdoctoral fellowships, the pressures of writing grant proposals and establishing a research laboratory take priority over teaching. When these students and postdocs eventually get faculty positions, however, few of them have experience in designing and teaching their own courses, and yet they are expected to teach right away.
