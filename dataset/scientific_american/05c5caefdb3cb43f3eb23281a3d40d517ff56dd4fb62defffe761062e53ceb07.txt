Read Our Latest Issue A look at strike capabilities worldwide, and how a bomb would affect single cities and people. Nine countries could kill many people on a moment's notice by launching missiles carrying nuclear warheads. A 10th, Iran, may be weaponizing uranium. The U.S., Russia and China can bomb virtually any country with long-range ballistic missiles and, along with France and the U.K., could do the same using submarines.
